Our Mission

Building decentralized identity infrastructure to empower African communities in Web3

Why We Exist

Africa faces a critical trust infrastructure gap. Over 500 million people lack formal identification, while talented creators and communities struggle to establish verifiable reputations in the digital economy.

ChainCred Africa provides the missing link - open-source, blockchain-based tools for identity, credentials, and reputation that work for Africa's unique needs.

Our 2025 Vision

To onboard 1 million Africans to Web3 through self-sovereign identity, enabling participation in decentralized finance, creator economies, and community governance - all while maintaining full control over their digital selves.

Africa-First Approach

Designed for mobile-first users, low-bandwidth environments, and local community structures.

Open Infrastructure

Fully open-source protocol that anyone can build upon or integrate with existing systems.

Community-Led

Governance by and for African users, with localized credentialing partners.
