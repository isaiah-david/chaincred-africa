What is ChainCred Africa?

ChainCred Africa is an open-source platform built on Ethereum to provide decentralized identity and trust infrastructure for African creators, students, cooperatives, and grassroots organizations.

It enables users to create wallet-based profiles, earn NFT credentials, and build verifiable onchain reputations. ChainCred supports transparency, local coordination, and digital trust in underserved regions.
